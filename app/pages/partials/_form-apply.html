Residency status

Current Australian Government legislation requires us to ensure all Coles team members have the right to work within Australia.

As part of this recruitment process we will ask you to provide consent for the use of your personal information for the purpose of confirming your legal entitlement to work in Australia.

You will be required to provide proof of your right to work in Australia if you are successful in gaining a position with Coles.

Proof of Australian working rights is demonstrated via the presentation of a Passport, or Australian birth certificate with photo ID, or Australian citizenship certificate with photo ID.
