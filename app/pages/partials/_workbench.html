What is Workbench?

Do you ever receive electronic resumes via email and think whats the fastest way to get these into PageUp? Create applicant profiles with ease and in bulk with drag & drop functionality. Multiple files can be dragged and dropped in one action. The information that is automatically populated for an applicant include:

  • The personal details - first name, last name, email address, phone number, address
  • Education history
  • Employment history
